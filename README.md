# 🌱 Imbewu AI

> **Plant the Future. Fund Farmers. Grow Together.**

Imbewu AI is a **Gemini-powered, climate-smart agricultural funding platform** that empowers small-scale farmers to access funding through AI-driven trust and transparency. By combining Google Gemini and Google Cloud technologies, Imbewu AI makes it easier for farmers to prove their progress, receive climate-smart guidance, and connect with potential funders.

Built for the **Google Cloud & Gemini Buildathon**, Imbewu AI demonstrates how artificial intelligence can improve financial inclusion, strengthen food security, and increase climate resilience across Africa.

---

## 🚜 The Problem

Millions of small-scale farmers across Africa have fertile land and the skills to grow food but struggle to secure funding.

Traditional financing is often inaccessible because:

* Financial institutions cannot easily verify farming progress.
* Investors lack visibility into how funds are being used.
* Climate change increases agricultural risk.
* Rural farmers often face digital literacy barriers.

This lack of trust limits investment, slows agricultural growth, and threatens food security.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Imbewu AI uses **Google Gemini** to create a trusted connection between farmers and funders.

Farmers can:

* Register using natural voice interactions.
* Upload images of their farmland and crops.
* Receive AI-generated crop health analysis.
* Access climate-smart farming recommendations.
* Track funding milestones throughout the growing season.

Investors can:

* View AI-verified farmer profiles.
* Monitor crop progress through visual analysis.
* Receive AI-generated progress summaries.
* Make more informed funding decisions.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🎙 Voice-First Farmer Registration

Farmers can register by speaking naturally, making the platform accessible for users with limited literacy or typing experience.

### 🤖 Gemini AI Understanding

Gemini extracts structured information from voice conversations, creating a farmer profile automatically.

### 📷 AI Crop Verification

Gemini Vision analyzes uploaded crop images to identify:

* Crop type
* Growth stage
* Crop health
* Potential drought stress
* Possible pest or disease indicators

### 🌦 Climate Assistant

Farmers receive AI-powered recommendations based on weather conditions and crop status, helping them adapt to climate challenges.

### 📊 Investor Dashboard

Investors can view:

* Farmer profiles
* Funding progress
* Crop verification status
* AI-generated summaries
* Crop health insights
* Milestone completion
